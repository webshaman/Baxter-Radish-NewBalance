Case Study

Virgin Voyages

The Ask

Find an artist to cover Culture Club's “Karma Chameleon” and star in a music video filmed on a Virgin Voyages cruise alongside Richard Branson.

What We Delivered
  • Produced a custom cover of “Karma Chameleon” featuring Drew Love.
  • Full-length music video at sea, with Drew Love on-camera with Richard Branson.
  • Campaign aired during the Super Bowl and Grammys (220M+ viewers).
  • Extended the campaign with a DSP release, Billboard charting, and an official Austin Millz remix.
  • The song peaked at No. 16 on the Billboard Adult Contemporary chart.
